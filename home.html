Welcome to Automotive essentials! This course covers everything form vehicle basics to advanced repair techniques, perfect for beginner and enthusiasts alike.

The automotive industry is constantly evolving, and is currently undergoing a transformation with the introduction of new technologies like robotics, additive manufacturing, and the Internet of Things. These technologies are paving the way for the development of autonomous vehicles.

About

Automotive technology is the study of how self-propelled vehicles move, and includes the design, creation, and repair of vehicle technology. Automotive technicians and related careers work on a variety of vehicles, including consumer vehicles, public transportation, heavy machinery, and heavy trucks.

Some skills that automotive technicians develop include: engine construction, fuel and ignition, brakes, power trains, and electronic and diagnostic equipment
